Pair Down Game Rules

The goal is to be the first player to shed all your cards.

Play a card that is either equal to or higher than the stockpile card, forms a natural pair (adds to 14), or is a 7.

Special actions:

  • 7: When a 7 is played, you immediately get another turn and can play any card.
  • 14: Playing a 14 collects the entire stockpile as 1 point per card (stockpile is preserved for scoring) and then play passes.
  • Natural Pair: If your played card plus the stockpile top equals 14, you skip the next player's turn.
